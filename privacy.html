Plain language summary: QUARVO helps you split purchases across your existing credit cards. We collect only what we need to provide that service, we never sell your data, and we never store your full card numbers — that's handled by Stripe, our PCI-compliant payment processor.

1. Who We Are

QUARVO ("we," "us," or "our") operates the web application available at quarvo.io. QUARVO is a Credit Combination Technology platform that enables users to split high-value purchases across multiple existing credit cards.

QUARVO is not a lender, bank, or financial institution. We do not issue credit, provide loans, or create new credit lines. We are a technology platform that facilitates the use of existing credit.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Email address (when you join the waitlist or create an account)
  • Name (when you create an account)
  • Payment method tokens provided by Stripe (never raw card numbers)
  • Purchase amounts and transaction details you enter when using our service

Information collected automatically

  • IP address and approximate location (city/state level)
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages visited, features used, and time spent on the platform
  • Referring URL and session data

Information we do NOT collect

  • Full credit card numbers (handled entirely by Stripe)
  • Social Security Numbers or government ID numbers
  • Your full credit history or credit score
  • Bank account credentials

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the QUARVO platform
  • Process split payment transactions across your connected cards
  • Send you service-related communications (transaction confirmations, support)
  • Send you product updates and early access notifications (you can opt out at any time)
  • Detect and prevent fraud and unauthorized access
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Analyze aggregate usage to improve our product

We will never use your information for purposes incompatible with those stated above without your explicit consent.

4. Payment Processing

All payment processing is handled by Stripe, Inc., a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment processor. When you add a credit card to QUARVO:

  • Your card details are transmitted directly to Stripe — QUARVO never sees or stores your full card number, CVV, or expiration date
  • Stripe returns a secure token that we use to reference your payment method
  • Stripe's processing fees apply to each transaction in addition to QUARVO's flat platform fee of $5.99 per split transaction
  • Stripe's privacy policy governs how they handle your payment data: stripe.com/privacy

Fee transparency: QUARVO charges a flat fee of $5.99 per split transaction. This is disclosed clearly before you confirm any split. Standard Stripe processing fees also apply and are passed through at cost.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share your information only in the following circumstances:

Service providers

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Netlify — web hosting and form collection
  • Loops — transactional and marketing email delivery
  • Resend — transactional email infrastructure

All service providers are contractually bound to use your data only as necessary to provide services to QUARVO and in compliance with applicable law.

Legal requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of QUARVO, our users, or others.

Business transfers

If QUARVO is acquired, merged, or sells substantially all of its assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies required for the platform to function (session management, security). We may also use analytics tools to understand how users interact with our platform.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the QUARVO platform.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you close your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 90 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or regulatory compliance (typically up to 7 years for financial transaction records).

Waitlist email addresses are retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Opt-out of marketing — unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the link in any email we send
  • Data portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • California residents (CCPA) — you have the right to know, delete, and opt out of the sale of personal information. QUARVO does not sell personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [email protected].

9. Children's Privacy

QUARVO is not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately at [email protected] and we will delete it promptly.
