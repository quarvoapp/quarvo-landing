Plain language summary: QUARVO helps you split purchases across your existing credit cards. We collect only what we need to provide that service, we never sell your data, and we never store your full card numbers — that's handled by Stripe. When you connect your bank via Plaid, we only read your available credit balance. We never access your transaction history, income, or bank account data.

1. Who We Are

QUARVO ("we," "us," or "our") operates the web application available at app.quarvo.io. QUARVO is a Credit Combination Technology platform that enables users to split high-value purchases across multiple existing credit cards.

QUARVO is not a lender, bank, or financial institution. We do not issue credit, provide loans, or create new credit lines. We are a technology platform that facilitates the use of existing credit.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Email address and name (when you create an account)
  • Payment method tokens provided by Stripe (never raw card numbers)
  • Purchase amounts and transaction details you enter when using our service

Financial data via Plaid (when you connect your bank)

  • Available credit balance on connected credit cards — read-only, in real time
  • Account names and last 4 digits for identification purposes
  • Account ownership information to verify the account belongs to you

Information collected automatically

  • IP address and approximate location (city/state level)
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages visited, features used, and session data

Information we do NOT collect

  • Full credit card numbers (handled entirely by Stripe)
  • Bank account credentials (handled entirely by Plaid)
  • Transaction history, income, or investment data via Plaid
  • Social Security Numbers or government ID numbers
  • Your full credit history or credit score

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the QUARVO platform
  • Read your available credit balance to calculate optimal split distributions (QuantumSplit™)
  • Process split payment transactions across your connected cards
  • Send you transaction confirmation emails with full split details
  • Detect and prevent fraud and unauthorized access
  • Maintain an immutable evidence log for chargeback protection (24-month retention)
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations

We will never use your information for purposes incompatible with those stated above without your explicit consent.

4. Payment Processing (Stripe)

All payment processing is handled by Stripe, Inc., a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment processor. When you add a credit card to QUARVO:

  • Your card details are transmitted directly to Stripe — QUARVO never sees or stores your full card number, CVV, or expiration date
  • Stripe returns a secure token that we use to reference your payment method
  • Stripe's privacy policy governs how they handle your payment data: stripe.com/privacy

Fee transparency: QUARVO charges a platform fee per split transaction, disclosed clearly before you confirm any split. Standard Stripe processing fees also apply and are shown upfront.

5. Financial Data (Plaid Integration)

QUARVO uses Plaid Technologies, Inc. ("Plaid") to read available credit balances from your connected credit card accounts. By connecting your bank through Plaid, you authorize QUARVO to receive read-only access to your credit card balance information.

What we access via Plaid

  • Available credit balance on connected credit cards (real-time)
  • Account names and last 4 digits for identification
  • Account ownership information to verify the account belongs to you

What we do NOT access via Plaid

  • Transaction history or spending data
  • Income or employment information
  • Checking or savings account (bank account) data
  • Investment portfolio or retirement account data
  • Any data not directly needed to calculate your credit split

How we use Plaid data

Your available credit balance data is used solely to power the QuantumSplit™ algorithm, which calculates the optimal distribution of your purchase across your connected cards. This data is never sold or shared with third parties for any purpose.

How we store Plaid data

  • Your Plaid connection credentials (access tokens) are encrypted with AES-256-GCM encryption before storage
  • Balance data is cached for up to 7 days and refreshed each time you initiate a split
  • Plaid access tokens that have been inactive for 90 days are automatically deleted
  • All Plaid data is stored on servers located in the United States

Your rights regarding Plaid data

  • Disconnect at any time — you can disconnect any connected bank from within the QUARVO app. Upon disconnection, your Plaid access token is immediately revoked and deleted from our systems
  • Data deletion — request full deletion of your Plaid data by emailing support@quarvo.io
  • Plaid Portal — manage all your Plaid connections at my.plaid.com

Plaid's Privacy Policy: Plaid's collection and use of your financial data is also governed by Plaid's Privacy Policy, available at plaid.com/legal/#privacy-policy. By connecting your bank, you also agree to Plaid's End User Privacy Policy.

6. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share your information only in the following circumstances:

Service providers

  • Stripe — payment processing (PCI-DSS Level 1)
  • Plaid — read-only access to credit card available balances
  • Supabase — database and authentication infrastructure
  • Vercel — web hosting and edge network
  • Resend — transactional email delivery

All service providers are contractually bound to use your data only as necessary to provide services to QUARVO and in compliance with applicable law.

Legal requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of QUARVO, our users, or others.

Business transfers

If QUARVO is acquired, merged, or sells substantially all of its assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email before your information is transferred.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies required for the platform to function (session management, security tokens). We do not use advertising cookies or sell your browsing data to third parties.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the QUARVO platform.

8. Data Retention

We retain different types of data for different periods based on legal requirements and operational necessity:

  • Plaid access tokens (active) — retained while your bank connection is active, maximum 24 months
  • Plaid access tokens (inactive/disconnected) — deleted within 90 days
  • Balance snapshots from Plaid — deleted after 7 days (stale data is not useful)
  • Split transaction records — retained for 7 years (financial record requirement)
  • Split evidence logs — retained for 24 months minimum (chargeback defense window)
  • Account data — retained until account deletion, then anonymized within 30 days

If you close your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or regulatory compliance.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal data. Submit a deletion request at any time by emailing support@quarvo.io with subject "Data Deletion Request"
  • Disconnect Plaid — revoke Plaid access for any connected bank at any time from within the app
  • Opt-out of marketing — unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time
  • Data portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • California residents (CCPA) — you have the right to know, delete, and opt out of the sale of personal information. QUARVO does not sell personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@quarvo.io.

10. Children's Privacy

QUARVO is not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately at support@quarvo.io and we will delete it promptly.

Your continued use of QUARVO after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
