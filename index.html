FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is this a new loan?
No. QUARVO doesn't lend you money. It helps you use the credit you already have across your existing cards. No new debt. No interest on new credit lines.
Do I need to open new accounts?
Never. QUARVO works with the cards already in your wallet. We just help them work together for the first time.
Will this affect my credit score?
No hard credit pulls. No new inquiries. Your credit profile stays exactly as it is — we're just optimizing how you use existing available credit.
What kinds of purchases can I make?
Any high-value purchase where one card's limit falls short — travel, electronics, furniture, experiences, medical, and more. If it matters, QUARVO can help.
How is QUARVO different from BNPL?
BNPL services like Klarna or Affirm give you new credit and charge interest or fees. QUARVO activates credit you already own. Fundamentally different — and fundamentally better.
When will it launch?
Quarvo is live now at app.quarvo.io. Create your first split today — no account needed to get started.
