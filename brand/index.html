About Quarvo

Boilerplate (short, ~50 words):

Quarvo is a payment infrastructure company that lets shoppers complete high-AOV purchases by splitting a single transaction across multiple credit cards they already hold. Built on Stripe Connect, Quarvo recovers declined high-ticket sales for merchants — without new debt for customers and without replatforming for merchants.

Boilerplate (long, ~120 words):

Quarvo is a payment infrastructure company solving one of the largest unmonetized cohorts in high-AOV e-commerce: the 6.5%+ of $1,500+ checkouts that decline because a single card doesn't have enough room — even when the customer's combined credit across all their cards comfortably does. Quarvo runs as a Stripe Connect platform that orchestrates atomic two-phase commits across N customer cards, recovering 50–70% of declined transactions at the moment of decline. Customers pay nothing extra, take on no new debt, and earn rewards on each card normally. Merchants pay 2% only on recovered transactions and never replatform — Quarvo settles in full through the merchant's existing Stripe account.

Clear space & minimum size

Maintain clear space equal to the height of the "Q" character around all sides of the logo. Minimum size: 24px tall for the wordmark, 16px tall for the mark only. Below those sizes the gradient compresses too tightly to read.

// DO
  • Use the gradient direction as provided (top-left violet → bottom-right cyan).
  • Keep at least 1× the Q-height of clear space around the logo.
  • Use the dark version on backgrounds darker than 50% lightness; light version on lighter.
  • Scale uniformly. SVGs preserve crispness at any size.
// DON'T
  • Don't recolor the gradient outside of the approved violet→cyan or violet→teal pairs.
  • Don't stretch or skew the logo. Preserve aspect ratio always.
  • Don't add effects like outer shadow, bevel, or extra strokes.
  • Don't place on busy backgrounds without enough contrast — gradients lose against gradients.
